Timestamp-api for FCC

Directions:

1. Append a date or a unix timestamp to the end of the url for this Heroku app ( like 'https://timestamp-ms.herokuapp.com/December%2015,%202015' or 'https://timestamp-ms.herokuapp.com/1450137600' and the app will return the Unix timestamp and the natural language form of that date.

What you should see:

{ unix: 1450137600, natural: "December 15, 2015" }
